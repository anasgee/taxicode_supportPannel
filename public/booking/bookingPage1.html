What is a Priority Account and how do I get one?

Priority accounts are reserved for customers who book with us on a regular basis. With a priority account, your bookings will get extra focus. From time to time you may receive free vehicle upgrades and on busy days when vehicle supply is limited, we will get you first. Priority accounts also get access to a dedicated account manager.


Can I have a credit account?

If you are a business, you can apply for a credit account. To apply for a credit account create a ticket on this support portal.


Am I charged if my flight is delayed?

No, we do not charge you any extra if your flight is delayed. The driver will keep an eye on the flight number and try to take any delays into account. However, the driver is not liable if he is unable to fulfil your journey due to a major flight delay or if unforeseen circumstances keep you in the airport for far longer than anticipated.


Can I book a vehicle with a child seat?

Should you require a car seat, please provide details in the notes box when making your booking and we will endeavour to meet your requirements. Some operators may charge you a small fee to provide a child or to store a child seat if you wish to use your own.
